CMPSC 457 Lab #2

By: Matthew Panetta

Instructions

In this lab I needed to:
  • Draw a white face in the center of the screen
  • Move the face around the screen with velocity variables
  • Have the face bounce off screen boundaries
  • Inverse the face's velocity after it has bounced off a boundary
  • Change the face's color after collision with boundary
  • Have the program terminate when 'q' is pressed, or when the RMB is clicked.
  • Maintain the face's size and shape regardless of screen size

My Implementation

  • I calculated the circle (for the head) by using a GL_POLYGON and splitting the circle into 30 different line segments.
  • The eyes also use the circle function as described above, while the mouth was made by a different GL_POLYGON (trapezoidal)
  • The face has an initial xVelocity of 0.02 and initial yVelocity of 0.03.
  • The face is drawn to screen, its position variables update, the face is cleared from the screen, and then redrawn.
  • Movement is simulated by adding xVelocity to the face's xPosition (likewise with the y) everytime the face is drawn.
  • The position variables are checked every draw to see if the face is about to go off the screen.
  • If the face's position is past the screen boundary, the cooresponding velocity is inverted.
  • The face changes color using three random numbers.
